# 🗺️ Maps of Content

> [!info] What is a MOC?
> A **Map of Content** is a note that links to other notes on a theme — a hand-built
> table of contents for a topic. MOCs are how you *navigate* a growing vault without
> rigid folders. Think of them as the index pages of your second brain.

## When to make a MOC
Create one when you have **~5+ notes** on a topic and want a hub to tie them together.
Use the **[[Templates/MOC|MOC template]]**.
