# 💡 Atomic notes beat long documents

Capturing **one idea per note** makes knowledge reusable. A note that mixes five ideas
can only be linked as a blob; five atomic notes can each be referenced, combined, and
recontextualized independently. Atomicity is what lets a second brain *compound* — small
pieces recombine into new thoughts you didn't plan in advance.

## Why it matters
- Each idea gets a stable address you can link to.
- Connecting ideas (the real value) becomes possible only when ideas are separable.
- Short notes are far cheaper to revisit and revise.
